design studio delta

This studio's focus is on the context of a library. This design tries to challenge digisited technologies and to reuse these elements into different, innovative typologies. Some of these include using the heat from data servers and to transfer this energy into hydro-heated slab technology. The site location is situated in Little Bourke street in China town on a vacant parking slot. The project was completed during COVID-19 through the University of Melbourne in 2020.

construction design

This studio has a strong approach to the technical aspect for building design. This includes the ability to read engineering drawings and to implement them into a design medium. The project is based off the Western BACE building and the subject was completed during 2020. All drawings were sourced from the architects and engineers who worked on the project during the building cycle.

epsilon journal

This Reflective Journal (RJ) is a repository of curated artefacts that describe my profile and identity, either developed or under development. This was part of the Epsilon subject in my final semester of university. It is different from a traditional ‘portfolio’and focuses on reflection and the learning journey over time, rather than simply showcasing skills and competencies in design.
